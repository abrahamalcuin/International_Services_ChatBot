## Deposit Refund
_Source: https://www.byui.edu/international-services/international-student-resources/deposit-refund_

International Student Services Graduating Students

Your deposit cannot be refunded while you are still actively attending BYU-Idaho. However, students can request a maximum of $200 withdrawn from their deposit per semester for any expenses. Please contact the Accounting Office to request this withdraw at 208-496-9610 or through email at accountingservices@byui.edu If you have paid your deposit but do not attend BYU-Idaho, you can request a refund, and it will be refunded to the person who originally paid the deposit. If you attend BYU-Idaho and graduate, transfer schools, withdraw, or leave for a mission, the remainder of your deposit can be refunded back to you. Please follow this link to fill out a Deposit Refund Request . All outstanding debt will be paid from your deposit before your refund is processed.
